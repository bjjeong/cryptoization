CRYPTOIZATION

Welcome!

Cryptoization is a data visualization of every Bitcoin (BTC) transaction that occurs on the blockchain in real time.


The size of each of the nodes are dependent on the size of the transaction, in USD.


Sit back, relax, and watch in awe as you witness the insane amount of money that moves throughout the world!



** Scroll/double-tap to zoom, click and hold on nodes to drag **
